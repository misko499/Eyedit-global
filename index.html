EyeDit develops visual production, photography, audiovisual media, rapid image services and creative communication. It represents the image engine of the holding and supports digital content, production services and brand storytelling.

Wellness

CCS CAN SS AM CAN SAM focuses on natural therapies, holistic health practices and alternative medicine frameworks. It brings the wellness axis into the holding with a more organic and human-centered energy.

EyeVision develops security, logistics, transport and telecom-oriented initiatives across terrestrial, maritime and aeronautical environments. It is the strategic systems arm of Eyedit Group.
